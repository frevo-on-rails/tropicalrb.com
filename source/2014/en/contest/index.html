Win a free ticket!

Compete for a ticket for the lectures of Abril Ruby Pro at April 26! Follow @AbrilProRuby's Twitter, write a tweet with the tag #aprb and mention the event Abril Pro Ruby somehow.

Each tweet is a coupon. The more you send, the more chance you have to win. Next Friday April 11, we will draw 2 tickets. Take this opportunity to watch the best Rubyists for free!

Rules

  1. The draw date will be on Friday, April 11, 2014, at noon (12:00 pm GMT-3)​​. It'll be 2 tickets to two different profiles.
  2. You must follow @AbrilProRuby's profile on Twitter.
  3. Each tweet must include the hashtag #aprb and mention the event Abril Pro Ruby somehow
  4. The randomly selected tweets will be readed and analyzed by the organizing committee.
  5. The use of bots that automatically generate tweets and content is prohibited.
  6. If the profile and tweet is not within the rules, it will be discarded and a new draw made.
  7. The winners will be announced by @AbrilProRuby's profile on the same day.
